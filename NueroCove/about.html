The NeuroCove Foundation

Founder

Paria Lajevardi

Founder/CEO · US

Having lived across three different countries, Paria has experienced both the opportunities and challenges of immigration firsthand. In 2025, she founded The NeuroCove Foundation to amplify immigrant voices and promote culturally inclusive mental health advocacy.

Why NeuroCove?

The name NeuroCove reflects our dual focus: the mind (neuro) and a place of refuge (cove). Immigrants carry rich inner worlds, memories of home, hopes for the future, and often unspoken struggles. We believe those inner worlds deserve care, attention, and community.

Through storytelling, advocacy, and resource building, we work to ensure that no immigrant feels alone in their journey.
